<p><strong>The Fuel-Grade Petcoke Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shot Coke Type</li><li>Sponge Coke Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel-grade petcoke market is categorized into shot coke type and sponge coke type. Shot coke type is characterized by small, round particles that are produced during the delayed coking process. On the other hand, sponge coke type consists of larger, irregularly shaped particles. Both types are used as fuel in industries such as cement, power generation, and steel production due to their high calorific value and low ash content. The choice between shot coke and sponge coke depends on the specific requirements of the end user.</p></p>
