The Vehicle Power Module is responsible for managing the overall power distribution in the vehicle. The Electric Drive Main Module controls the electric drive system, including motor speed and torque. The Auxiliary Module supports additional functions such as regenerative braking and battery management. These different modules work together to efficiently control the electric drive system in vehicles.</p></p>
