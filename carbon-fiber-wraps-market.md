<p><ul><li>Rayon Based</li><li>Pitch Based</li><li>Pan Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon fiber wraps are classified into three types based on the precursor material used in their production: Rayon Based, Pitch Based, and Pan Based. Rayon Based carbon fiber wraps are made from cellulose fibers, Pitch Based wraps are derived from coal tar or petroleum pitches, and Pan Based wraps are produced from polyacrylonitrile fibers. Each type of carbon fiber wrap offers unique properties and characteristics, making them suitable for various applications such as aerospace, automotive, and sports equipment.</p></p>
