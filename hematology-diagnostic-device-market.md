<p><p>Hematology diagnostic devices are medical instruments used to analyze and diagnose blood-related diseases and disorders. The increasing prevalence of blood disorders such as anemia, leukemia, and various types of cancers has primarily contributed to the market growth. This integration has led to faster turnaround times, reduced errors, and improved patient care.</p><p>Furthermore, there is a growing focus on the development and adoption of point-of-care (POC) hematology diagnostic devices.
