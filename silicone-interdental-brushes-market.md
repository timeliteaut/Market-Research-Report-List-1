<p><p>Silicone interdental brushes are available in three main market types based on their sizes: below 0.6mm, 0.6mm-1.2mm, and above 1.2mm. Brushes below 0.6mm are best suited for tight spaces between teeth, offering precision cleaning. Brushes in the 0.6mm-1.2mm range are versatile and suitable for most interdental spaces. Brushes above 1.2mm are ideal for larger gaps and bridgework. Each size caters to different interdental needs, providing effective cleaning and maintenance for overall oral health.</p></p>
