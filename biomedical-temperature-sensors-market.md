<p><p>The biomedical temperature sensors market includes various types of sensors such as thermocouples, thermistors, fiber optic sensors, infrared sensors, and liquid crystal temperature sensors. Thermocouples are composed of two different metals that generate a voltage proportional to the temperature difference. Thermistors consist of temperature-sensitive resistors that change their resistance with temperature. Fiber optic sensors use light to measure temperature changes. Infrared sensors detect temperature by sensing infrared radiation emitted from an object. Liquid crystal temperature sensors use the changes in liquid crystal colors to determine temperature variations.</p></p>
