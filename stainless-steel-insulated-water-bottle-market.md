<p><strong>The Stainless Steel Insulated Water Bottle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Vacuum Stainless Steel Thermos Cup</li><li>Vacuum Stainless Steel Thermos Cup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Stainless Steel Insulated Water Bottle market includes two main types: Non-Vacuum Stainless Steel Thermos Cup and Vacuum Stainless Steel Thermos Cup. Non-Vacuum Thermos Cups are designed with a single wall construction, providing basic insulation to keep beverages hot or cold. Vacuum Thermos Cups, on the other hand, feature a double-wall construction with a vacuum-sealed layer in between, offering superior temperature retention. Vacuum cups are typically more effective at maintaining the desired temperature of the beverage for longer periods of time compared to non-vacuum cups.</p></p>
