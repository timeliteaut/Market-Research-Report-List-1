<p><p>Two-part epoxies are primarily classified into two main types: epoxy resin/amine and epoxy resin/hardener. Epoxy resin/amine formulations are typically used for their excellent adhesion, flexibility, and corrosion resistance properties. On the other hand, epoxy resin/hardener formulations are known for their high strength, durability, and ability to withstand extreme environments. Additionally, there are other niche markets for two-part epoxies, including specialty formulations for specific industries or applications such as aerospace, automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
