<p><ul><li>Horizontal Closed Tank</li><li>Silo/ Vertical Closed Tank</li><li>Open Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Milk storage tanks are available in three main types: horizontal closed tanks, silo/vertical closed tanks, and open tanks. Horizontal closed tanks are designed for large-scale dairy operations and are highly efficient for milk storage. Silo/vertical closed tanks have a tall and narrow shape, making them ideal for limited floor space. Open tanks are more commonly found in smaller dairy farms due to their cost-effectiveness. Each type of tank offers different benefits depending on the size and scale of the dairy operation.</p></p>
