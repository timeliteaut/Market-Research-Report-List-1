<p><strong>The FEP Tubing Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Shrinkable Tubing</li><li>Extruded Tubing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FEP tubing material market consists of two main types: heat shrinkable tubing and extruded tubing. Heat shrinkable tubing contracts in diameter when exposed to heat, providing a protective covering for wires and cables. Extruded tubing is formed by pushing softened FEP material through a die to create a specific shape and size. Both types of tubing are used in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and medical devices for their excellent chemical resistance, high temperature performance, and electrical insulation properties.</p></p>
