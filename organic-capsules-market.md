<p><p>The organic capsules market includes two types: hard capsules and soft capsules. Hard capsules are made from gelatin or vegetable materials and have a rigid outer shell. Both types of capsules are preferred by consumers who seek organic and natural alternatives in their supplements or medications.</p></p>
