<p><p>Commercial vehicle bearings market can be categorized into two main types: Ball Bearings and Roller Bearings. Ball bearings are known for their ability to reduce friction and provide high precision, making them suitable for applications requiring high speed and accuracy. On the other hand, Roller bearings are designed to support heavy radial and axial loads, making them ideal for tough working conditions in commercial vehicles. Both types of bearings play a critical role in ensuring smooth operation and longevity of commercial vehicles.</p></p>
