<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Self-dimming Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Self-dimming Mirror market includes two main types: Outer Self-dimming Mirrors and Inside Self-dimming Mirrors. Outer Self-dimming Mirrors are designed to reduce glare from headlights and other bright lights from behind the vehicle, improving visibility for the driver. Inside Self-dimming Mirrors are positioned inside the vehicle and adjust automatically to reduce glare from headlights and bright sun, enhancing the driver's comfort and safety. Both types of self-dimming mirrors are essential features in modern electric vehicles to optimize visibility and driving experience.</p></p>
