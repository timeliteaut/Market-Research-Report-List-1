<p><p>The Enterprise Content Management (ECM) System market can be categorized into two types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. Cloud-Based ECM systems offer flexibility and scalability by storing data on remote servers accessed through the internet. They reduce the need for hardware investments and enable remote access and collaboration. Conversely, On-Premises ECM systems involve storing data locally within the organization's infrastructure. This offers greater control over data security and customization options but requires higher upfront investments and maintenance costs compared to cloud-based systems.</p></p>
