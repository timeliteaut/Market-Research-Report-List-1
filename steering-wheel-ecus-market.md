<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Steering Wheel ECUs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>REPS</li><li>CEPS</li><li>PEPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steering Wheel ECUs market can be categorized into three main types: REPS (Redundant Electronic Power Steering), CEPS (Column Electric Power Steering), and PEPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering). REPS systems use redundant electronic components for added safety and reliability. CEPS systems integrate the power steering system directly into the steering column for compactness. PEPS systems, on the other hand, use a pinion system for more precise steering control. Each type offers unique features and advantages to meet different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
