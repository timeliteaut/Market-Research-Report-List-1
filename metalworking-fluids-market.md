<p><p>Metalworking fluids are essential in various industrial processes to improve the efficiency and quality of metal working operations. There are four main types of metalworking fluids: metal removal fluids, metal forming fluids, metal protecting fluids, and metal treating fluids. Metal removal fluids assist in removing metal chips during cutting operations, metal forming fluids aid in shaping metal components, metal protecting fluids prevent corrosion, and metal treating fluids improve the physical and chemical properties of metal surfaces. Each type of fluid plays a vital role in ensuring the smooth functioning of metalworking processes.</p></p>
