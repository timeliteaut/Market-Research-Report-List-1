<p><p>Cement silo market is divided into Type I and Type II segments based on their applications and design features. Type I silos are commonly used for storing dry bulk materials like cement, fly ash, lime, and sand in construction projects. They are typically made of welded steel and have a larger storage capacity. On the other hand, Type II silos are designed for specialty applications such as storing grain, food products, or chemicals. They are usually made of stainless steel or aluminum and have stricter safety and hygiene requirements.</p></p>
