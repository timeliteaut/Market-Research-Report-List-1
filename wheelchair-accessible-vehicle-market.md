<p><p>Wheelchair Accessible Vehicles come in two main market types: Side Entry and Rear Entry. Side Entry vehicles allow wheelchair users to enter and exit through the side of the vehicle, typically through a ramp or lift located on the side of the vehicle. Rear Entry vehicles, on the other hand, allow wheelchair users to enter and exit through the rear of the vehicle, often through a ramp or lift located at the back. Each type offers unique benefits and considerations for wheelchair users and caregivers.</p></p>
