X-ray blood irradiation uses X-ray technology to irradiate blood products, while gamma-ray blood irradiation uses gamma-ray technology. Both types of irradiation effectively prevent graft-versus-host disease by destroying T cells in the blood products without compromising the quality of the blood products.</p></p>
