<p><p>Flexographic printing self-adhesive labels are available in two main types: release liner labels and liner-less labels. Release liner labels have a backing material that is peeled off before applying the label, while liner-less labels do not have a liner and are simply applied directly onto the surface. Release liner labels are more common and offer easy application, while liner-less labels are environmentally friendly and reduce waste. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the market for self-adhesive labels.</p></p>
