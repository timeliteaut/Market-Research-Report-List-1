<p><p>Air springs for vehicles are essential components that provide cushioning and stability to vehicles, particularly in heavy-duty applications. There are three main types of air springs on the market: convoluted, sleeves, and others. Convoluted air springs feature a bellows-like design that allows for greater flexibility and durability. Sleeve air springs consist of a cylindrical sleeve that expands and contracts to absorb shocks and vibrations. Other types of air springs may include rolling lobe or tapered designs, offering variations in performance and application suitability.</p></p>
