<p><p>Cantilever Reclaimer is a type of machine used in material handling to recover bulk materials from a stockpile. There are two main market types: Wheel and Chain. Wheel Cantilever Reclaimers have a rotating wheel with buckets attached to reclaim materials. They are suitable for lighter materials and smaller stockpiles. Chain Cantilever Reclaimers use a chain with scrapers to recover materials and are ideal for handling heavier and more abrasive materials. Both types offer efficient and reliable performance in various industries such as mining, power plants, and ports.</p></p>
