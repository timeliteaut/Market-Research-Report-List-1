<p><p>Specialty films market is segmented into different types such as polyester, fluoropolymer, nylon, polyimide, polycarbonate, and others. Polyester films are known for their high tensile strength and durability. Fluoropolymer films are resistant to heat, chemicals, and UV radiation. Nylon films offer high puncture resistance and flexibility. Polyimide films are known for their high temperature resistance and electrical insulation properties. Polycarbonate films are transparent and offer exceptional impact resistance. Other specialty films include materials like acrylic, polypropylene, and PVC.</p></p>
