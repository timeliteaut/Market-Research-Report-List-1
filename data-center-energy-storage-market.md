<p><strong>The Data Center Energy Storage Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Batteries</li><li>Super Capacitors</li><li>Flywheels</li><li>Compressed Air Energy Storage (CAES)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Center Energy Storage market encompasses various types of technologies used for storing energy. These include batteries, which store energy chemically and are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of integration. Super capacitors store energy electrostatically and provide a quick burst of power, making them suitable for short-duration high-power applications. Flywheels store energy mechanically in a rotating mass and are preferred for their high cycling efficiency. Compressed Air Energy Storage (CAES) stores energy by compressing air and releasing it when needed. Other storage technologies also exist, such as thermal energy storage and fuel cells.</p></p>
