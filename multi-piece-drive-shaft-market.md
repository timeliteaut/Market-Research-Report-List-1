<p><ul><li>Rigid Shaft</li><li>Hollow Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-piece drive shaft market can be categorized into two main types: rigid shafts and hollow shafts. Rigid shafts are solid, continuous structures that provide high levels of strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, hollow shafts are lightweight and have a lower moment of inertia, making them suitable for applications where weight reduction is key. Both types of drive shafts play crucial roles in ensuring efficient power transmission in various industries.</p></p>
