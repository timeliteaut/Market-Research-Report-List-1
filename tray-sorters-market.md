<p><p>Tray sorters are used in distribution centers and warehouses for efficiently sorting and transporting items. Push tray sorters utilize trays that are pushed along a track to their designated location. Tilt tray sorters use trays that tilt to drop items into the desired chute. Split tray sorters have trays that split into two sections to allow for sorting in multiple directions. Other types of tray sorters may include loop tray sorters, vertical tray sorters, or carousel tray sorters, each offering unique benefits for specific sorting needs.</p></p>
