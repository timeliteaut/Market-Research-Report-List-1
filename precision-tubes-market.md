<p><p>Precision tubes are a crucial component in various industries due to their high-quality and precise dimensions. The market for precision tubes can be classified into two main types: seamless tubes and welded tubes. Seamless tubes are manufactured by extruding a solid metal billet through a die to form a tube without any seams, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. On the other hand, welded tubes are made from strips of metal that are welded together to form a tube, making them more cost-effective but with slightly lower precision than seamless tubes.</p></p>
