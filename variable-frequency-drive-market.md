<p><p>Variable frequency drives (VFDs) are classified into three main types based on their application - AC Drives, DC Drives, and Servo Drives. AC Drives are used for controlling the speed of AC electric motors. DC Drives are used for controlling the speed of DC electric motors. Servo Drives are used for precise control of position, velocity, and torque in machines. Each type of drive offers unique features and benefits, catering to different industrial applications and requirements.</p></p>
