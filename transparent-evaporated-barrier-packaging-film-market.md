<p><p>Transparent evaporated barrier packaging films are widely used in the food and beverage industry due to their ability to provide high barrier properties against oxygen and moisture. There are two main types of barrier coatings used in these films - AlOx type and SiOx type. AlOx type films are coated with aluminum oxide, providing excellent oxygen barrier properties, while SiOx type films are coated with silicon oxide, offering superior moisture barrier properties. Both types of films are popular choices for packaging perishable goods and extending their shelf life.</p></p>
