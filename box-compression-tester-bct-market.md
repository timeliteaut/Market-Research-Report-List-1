<p><p>Box Compression Tester (BCT) is a device used to measure the compressive strength of corrugated boxes. There are two main types of BCT: Pneumatic and Mechanical. Pneumatic BCTs use air pressure to apply force to the box, while Mechanical BCTs use mechanical components such as gears and levers. Both types of BCTs are used in various industries such as packaging, manufacturing, and logistics to ensure the quality and durability of corrugated boxes. The choice between Pneumatic and Mechanical BCTs depends on factors such as testing requirements and budget.</p></p>
