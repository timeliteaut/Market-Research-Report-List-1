<p><p>Acaricides market types include Organochlorine, Organophosphorus, Natural, and Others. Organochlorine acaricides are synthetic chemicals known for their persistence in the environment. Natural acaricides are derived from natural sources like plants or minerals and are considered safer for the environment. The Others category includes various types of acaricides not falling into the previous three groups, such as carbamates or pyrethroids.
