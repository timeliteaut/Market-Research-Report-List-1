<p><p>Injection influenza vaccine, also known as a flu shot, is a vaccine that helps protect against various strains of influenza viruses. The healthcare industry is also witnessing advancements in vaccine technology, leading to the development of more effective and targeted vaccines. This has contributed to the market growth as well.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing preference for quadrivalent influenza vaccines, which provide protection against four different strains of the flu virus compared to trivalent vaccines that protect against three strains.
