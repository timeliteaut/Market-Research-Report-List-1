<p><p>Timesheet management software can be categorized into two market types: on-premise and cloud-based. </p><p>On-premise software refers to installations that are hosted on a company's internal servers and require physical infrastructure and maintenance. With on-premise solutions, businesses have complete control over their data and security but may face higher upfront costs and limited accessibility.</p><p>Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is accessed and managed through the internet. This market type requires no hardware investment and provides automatic updates and backups.</p></p>
