<p><p>Automatic banknote sorters can be categorized into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic banknote sorters are designed to handle large volumes of banknotes without any manual intervention, offering high-speed processing efficiency. On the other hand, semi-automatic banknote sorters require some level of manual operation, such as the feeding of banknotes into the machine. While fully automatic sorters are ideal for businesses with high cash volumes, semi-automatic sorters are more suitable for smaller operations with less frequent sorting needs.</p></p>
