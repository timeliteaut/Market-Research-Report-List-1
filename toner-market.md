On the other hand, chemical toner is a newer type of toner made from liquid chemicals that are dried onto paper using heat and pressure. Chemical toner is known for its ability to produce high-quality prints with vibrant colors. Both traditional and chemical toner markets cater to the printing industry, but chemical toner is gaining popularity due to its advanced technology.</p></p>
