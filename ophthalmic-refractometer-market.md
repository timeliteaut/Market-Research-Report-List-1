<p><p>Ophthalmic refractometers are used to determine an individual's eyeglass prescription. The market for ophthalmic refractometers can be categorized into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic refractometers are fully automated machines that quickly and accurately measure a patient's refractive error without much manual intervention. On the other hand, semi-automatic refractometers require some manual adjustment by the technician before taking measurements. Both types of refractometers are essential tools in optometric practices for providing precise and efficient eye care services.</p></p>
