<p><p>Freeze drying and lyophilization dryers are used to remove moisture from substances while preserving their structure and properties. Tray style freeze dryers use trays to hold the substance while the moisture is removed. Manifold freeze dryers use a series of interconnected tubes to freeze dry the substance.
