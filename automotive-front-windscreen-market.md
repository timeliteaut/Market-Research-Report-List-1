<p><p>The automotive front windscreen market is divided into two main types: laminated glass and tempered glass. Laminated glass consists of two layers of glass with a layer of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) in between, offering enhanced safety and protection from impact. Tempered glass is created through a process of extreme heating and rapid cooling, resulting in increased strength and resistance to breakage. Both types of glass are widely used in the automotive industry for their durability and safety features.</p></p>
