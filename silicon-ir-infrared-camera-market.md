<p><strong>The Silicon IR (Infrared) Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Near Infrared</li><li>Short-Wave Infrared</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared</li><li>Long-Wave Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicon IR (Infrared) cameras are classified into different types based on the wavelength range they operate in. Near Infrared cameras have a wavelength range of 0.7 to 1.5 microns, making them suitable for applications like surveillance and agriculture. Short-Wave Infrared cameras operate in the 1.5 to 3 micron range, useful for solar cell inspection and moisture detection. Mid-Wave Infrared cameras cover the 3 to 5 micron range, ideal for gas analysis and thermal imaging. Long-Wave Infrared cameras operate in the 8 to 14 micron range, commonly used for thermography and security applications.</p></p>
