<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Telematics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plug and Play Telematics</li><li>Hardwired Install Telematics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plug and Play Telematics for electric vehicles involve easy installation without the need for wiring or professional assistance. Users can simply plug the device into the vehicle's OBD-II port for instant access to real-time data and insights. On the other hand, Hardwired Install Telematics requires a more complex installation process, involving wiring the device directly into the vehicle's electrical system. This type of telematics provides more permanent and reliable monitoring capabilities for electric vehicles.</p></p>
