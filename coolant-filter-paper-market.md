<p><p>Coolant filter paper is used in various types of coolant filtration systems to remove impurities from the coolant fluid. Vacuum coolant filter paper utilizes a vacuum pressure system to draw coolant through the filter paper, while gravity coolant filter paper relies on the force of gravity to push the coolant through the filter paper. Both types of filter papers are essential in maintaining the cleanliness and efficiency of coolant systems in industrial applications.</p></p>
