<p><p>Plastic coating for automotive includes three main types: primer, base coat, and clearcoat. The primer is applied as the first layer to improve adhesion and corrosion resistance. The base coat provides the color and appearance of the coating. The clearcoat is the final layer that adds gloss, protection, and durability. Each type plays a crucial role in providing a high-quality finish for automotive parts, enhancing aesthetics, and extending the lifespan of the coating.</p></p>
