<p><strong>The Full Colour LED Displays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD</li><li>COB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full Colour LED Displays are available in different types such as SMD (Surface Mount Device) and COB (Chip on Board). SMD displays consist of individual LED chips mounted onto a circuit board, offering high resolution and brightness. On the other hand, COB displays feature multiple LED chips directly mounted onto a substrate, providing better heat dissipation and cost efficiency. Both types cater to various market needs for indoor and outdoor advertising, sports events, entertainment, and information display applications.</p></p>
