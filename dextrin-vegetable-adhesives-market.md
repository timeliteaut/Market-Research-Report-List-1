<p><p>Dextrin vegetable adhesives are widely used in various industries such as paper and packaging, textiles, and pharmaceuticals. They can be classified into two main types: white dextrin and yellow dextrin. </p><p>White dextrin is a water-soluble adhesive derived from starch. It has a light color and is commonly used for applications that require a clear product, such as in tube winding, paper bag manufacturing, and envelope making.</p><p>Yellow dextrin, on the other hand, has a darker shade and is used for applications like corrugated board lamination, paper labeling, and carton sealing. It provides enhanced adhesion and bonding strength.</p><p>Both white and yellow dextrin adhesives offer efficient and cost-effective solutions for a wide range of industries, providing reliable adhesion properties to meet the specific requirements of different applications.</p></p>
