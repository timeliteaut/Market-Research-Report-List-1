<p><strong>The Dip Welded Wire Mesh Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro Galvanized Welded Wire Mesh</li><li>Hot Dipped Galvanized Steel Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dip welded wire mesh market includes two main types: Electro Galvanized Welded Wire Mesh and Hot Dipped Galvanized Steel Mesh. Electro galvanized mesh is coated with a thin layer of zinc through electrolysis, offering corrosion resistance. On the other hand, hot-dipped galvanized steel mesh is immersed in molten zinc, providing a thicker and more durable zinc coating. Both types are widely used in construction, agriculture, and industrial applications for fencing, partitioning, and reinforcement due to their strength, durability, and rust resistance.</p></p>
