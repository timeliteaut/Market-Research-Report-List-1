<p><p>Building-Integrated Photovoltaic Skylights are a type of skylight integrated with solar panels that generate electricity from sunlight. There are two main types of photovoltaic panels used in these skylights: Crystalline Panel and Thin Film Panel. Crystalline panels are made from silicon crystals and are more efficient but expensive, while thin film panels are lighter, flexible, and cheaper to produce. Both types offer sustainable energy solutions for buildings while providing natural light and reducing electricity costs.</p></p>
