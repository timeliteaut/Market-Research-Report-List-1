<p><strong>The Chrome Plated Metal Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Chromium Powder</li><li>Electrolytic Chromium Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chrome plated metal powder market includes two main types - Metal Chromium Powder and Electrolytic Chromium Powder. Metal Chromium Powder is obtained through the reduction of chromite ore in the presence of carbon, resulting in a fine powder that is used for chrome plating applications. On the other hand, Electrolytic Chromium Powder is produced through the electrolysis of chromium chloride solution, resulting in a higher purity powder that is often used in specialty applications requiring a high level of purity.</p></p>
