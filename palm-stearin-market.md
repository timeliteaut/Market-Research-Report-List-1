<p><p>Palm stearin is a type of fat derived from the oil palm tree. It is commonly used in various industries, such as food, cosmetics, and biodiesel production. There are two main types of palm stearin: soft stearin and hard stearin. Soft stearin has a lower melting point and is often used in products that require a creamy texture, like confectionery items and spreads. On the other hand, hard stearin has a higher melting point and is commonly utilized in applications that require solid fats, such as candles, soaps, and margarines.</p></p>
