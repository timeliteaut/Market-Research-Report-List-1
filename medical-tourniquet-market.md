<p><p>Medical tourniquets are available in different types, with latex and TPE tourniquets being two common options on the market. Latex tourniquets are made from natural rubber and are known for their durability and elasticity. On the other hand, TPE tourniquets are made from thermoplastic elastomer and offer similar stretch and flexibility as latex tourniquets without the risk of latex allergies. Both types of tourniquets are widely used in medical settings for various procedures requiring temporary restriction of blood flow.</p></p>
