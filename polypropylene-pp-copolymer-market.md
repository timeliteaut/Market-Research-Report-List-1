<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) Copolymer market includes various types such as Isotactic Polypropylene, Atactic Polypropylene, and Syndiotactic Polypropylene. Isotactic PP has a high degree of crystallinity and is stiff and strong, making it suitable for applications requiring strength and rigidity. Atactic PP is an amorphous material with low melting point, used in applications requiring flexibility. Syndiotactic PP has a crystalline structure but with different orientation of polymer chains, offering a balance of stiffness and impact resistance. Each type caters to specific needs in different industries.</p></p>
