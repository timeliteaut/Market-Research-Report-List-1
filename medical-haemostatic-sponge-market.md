<p><p>A Medical Haemostatic Sponge is a sterile, absorbable sponge used in surgical procedures to control bleeding. These sponges are typically made from natural biopolymers such as collagen or gelatin, which stimulate the body's clotting process and promote the formation of blood clots at the site of bleeding. They come in various forms such as powder, gauze, or sponge, and can be easily applied to wounds or surgical sites.</p><p>The Medical Haemostatic Sponge Market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The market is being driven by several factors, including the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, the rise in surgical procedures, and the growing aging population. Additionally, advancements in technology and product innovations in the field of hemostatic agents are also contributing to market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the market growth can be attributed to the rising demand for minimally invasive surgeries, as these procedures require effective hemostatic agents to control bleeding. The increasing adoption of medical haemostatic sponges by healthcare professionals is also fueling market growth. These sponges are cost-effective, easy to use, and have shown promising results in reducing bleeding time during surgical procedures.</p><p>In terms of latest trends, there is a growing trend towards the development of hemostatic sponges made from synthetic materials, such as oxidized regenerated cellulose and polyvinyl alcohol.
