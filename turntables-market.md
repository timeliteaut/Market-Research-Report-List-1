<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Turntables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-drive Turntable</li><li>Belt-drive Turntable</li><li>Idler-wheel Turntable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct-drive turntables utilize a motor to directly spin the platter, providing precise and consistent speed control ideal for DJing and professional use. Belt-drive turntables use a belt to connect the motor to the platter, reducing motor noise and vibration for high-fidelity audio playback. Idler-wheel turntables use a rubber wheel to transfer motion from the motor to the platter, offering a unique sound quality favored by audiophiles. Each market type caters to different consumer preferences based on performance, sound quality, and intended use.</p></p>
