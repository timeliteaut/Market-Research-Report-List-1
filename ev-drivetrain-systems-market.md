<p><p>EV drivetrain systems are classified into two main types: Battery Electric Vehicle (BEV) and Hybrid Electric Vehicle (HEV) drivetrains. BEV drivetrains rely solely on electric power stored in batteries to operate, offering zero emissions and high efficiency. HEV drivetrains combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, providing improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions compared to traditional gasoline vehicles. Both types are becoming increasingly popular as consumers and governments look for cleaner and more sustainable transportation options.</p></p>
