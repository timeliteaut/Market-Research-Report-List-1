<p><p>Automotive tactile switches come in various types to suit different needs. Standard types are the basic switches used in most automotive applications. Illuminated types include switches with LED lights for visibility in the dark. Sealed types are designed to prevent dust and moisture from affecting switch performance. SMD types are surface mount switches that are easy to install on printed circuit boards. Other types may include custom-designed switches for specific automotive functions. Each type caters to different requirements within the automotive industry.</p></p>
