<p><p>Silicon Carbide (SiC) Market is segmented into three main types: 3C-SiC, 4H-SiC, and 6H-SiC. 3C-SiC is cubic crystal structure and has potential for use in power devices. 4H-SiC has a hexagonal crystal structure and is widely used in semiconductor and automotive industries for its high thermal conductivity. 6H-SiC is another hexagonal crystal structure variant that is preferred for electronic devices due to its lower stacking faults. Each type offers unique properties and applications within the SiC market.</p></p>
