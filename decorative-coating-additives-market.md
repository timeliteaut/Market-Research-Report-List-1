<p><strong>The Decorative Coating Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based & Powder Based Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Decorative coating additives market types include water-based, solvent-based, powder-based systems, and others. Water-based additives are environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and have low VOC content. Solvent-based additives offer fast drying times and high durability. Powder-based systems are known for their high resistance to chemicals and abrasion. Other additives in the market may include UV-curable, high-solid, or radiation-curable options. Each type of additive offers unique properties and benefits, catering to different preferences and requirements in the decorative coating industry.</p></p>
