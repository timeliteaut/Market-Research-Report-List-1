<p><p>Performance glass paint market offers various types of coatings such as pyrolytic coating, sputtered coating, screen coating, spray coating, and others. Pyrolytic coating involves depositing ceramic material onto glass at high temperatures for durability. Sputtered coating uses a thin film of metal oxide for energy efficiency. Screen coating uses silk screens to apply patterns or images onto glass. Spray coating applies paint through a nozzle for quick and even coverage. Other types include electrochromic and self-cleaning coatings for specialized applications.</p></p>
