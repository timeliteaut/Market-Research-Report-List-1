<p><p>Galvanized bolts come in various types to cater to different needs in construction and industrial applications. Hex bolts are the most common type with a hexagonal head for easy tightening with a wrench. Carriage bolts have a smooth, dome-shaped head for a neat finish and are often used in wood applications. Lag bolts are heavy-duty bolts with a coarse thread designed for securing heavy materials to wood. Other types of galvanized bolts include anchor bolts, elevator bolts, and eye bolts, each serving specific purposes in construction and fastening.</p></p>
