<p><p>Orthopedic regenerative surgical market offers different types of treatments, including allografts, cell-based, viscosupplements, and synthetics. Allografts involve using tissue from a donor to repair damaged areas. Cell-based treatment involves using stem cells to promote tissue regeneration. Viscosupplements entail injecting lubricating substances into joints to improve movement. Synthetics treatments involve using artificial materials to aid in tissue repair. Each of these treatments plays a unique role in promoting healing and providing relief for orthopedic issues.</p></p>
