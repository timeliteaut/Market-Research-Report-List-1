<p><p>Hand washing machines can be classified into two types: wire type and battery type. Wire type machines require a physical connection to a power source, such as a plug, to function. These machines provide a constant power supply, ensuring consistent washing performance. On the other hand, battery type machines are portable and do not require a direct power connection. They operate using rechargeable batteries, making them convenient for outdoor use or areas with limited access to electricity. Both types offer efficient hand washing solutions, catering to different needs and environments.</p></p>
