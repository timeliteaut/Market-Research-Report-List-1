<p><p>Wall-hung toilets come in two main market types: flush toilets and siphon toilets. Flush toilets use a flushing mechanism to dispose of waste by sending it through a pipe into the sewage system. Siphon toilets, on the other hand, use a siphoning action to remove waste by pulling it down through the drain. Both types offer advantages in terms of water efficiency and space-saving qualities, making them popular choices for consumers looking for modern and convenient bathroom solutions.</p></p>
