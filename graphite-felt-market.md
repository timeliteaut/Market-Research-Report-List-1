<p><p>Graphite felt can be categorized into two main types based on their physical properties: soft felt and rigid felt. Soft felt is characterized by its flexibility and ability to conform to irregular surfaces, making it ideal for applications where flexibility is required. Rigid felt, on the other hand, is stiff and maintains its shape well, making it suitable for applications where structural integrity is essential. Both types of graphite felt cater to different market needs and applications within industries like aerospace, energy storage, and chemical processing.</p></p>
