<p><strong>The Thermoelectric Air Conditioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Peltier Air Conditioner</li><li>Peltier Liquid to Air Conditioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoelectric air conditioners are divided into two main types: Peltier air conditioners and Peltier liquid to air conditioners. Peltier air conditioners use the Peltier effect to create a temperature difference between two electrical junctions, which results in cooling. On the other hand, Peltier liquid to air conditioners use a liquid to transfer heat away from the hot side of the Peltier module, providing efficient cooling. Both types offer unique advantages and are commonly used in various applications for cooling purposes.</p></p>
