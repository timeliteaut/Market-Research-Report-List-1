<p><strong>The Beer Dispense Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Draw System</li><li>Air Cooled System</li><li>Glycol Cooled System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Beer dispense systems come in three main types: Direct Draw System, Air Cooled System, and Glycol Cooled System. Direct Draw Systems are the simplest and most economical, with beer kegs stored directly below the tap. Air Cooled Systems use a fan to keep beer lines cool, while Glycol Cooled Systems use a separate refrigeration unit to chill a glycol solution that runs through the beer lines. Each system has its own benefits and is suitable for different types of establishments based on their size, budget, and specific needs.</p></p>
