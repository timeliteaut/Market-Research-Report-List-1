<p><p>Cloud block storage market refers to the various types of storage systems available in cloud computing environments. Locally-attached storage refers to storage devices directly connected to a single server or system, providing high performance and low latency. Network-attached storage, on the other hand, refers to storage devices connected to a network and accessed by multiple servers or systems. It offers centralized storage management and can be scaled easily. Both types play a significant role in cloud computing by offering secure, flexible, and scalable storage solutions to businesses and individuals.</p></p>
