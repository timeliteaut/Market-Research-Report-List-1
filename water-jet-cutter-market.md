<p><strong>The Water-jet Cutter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water-jet cutter market can be divided into Type I and Type II categories. Type I water-jet cutters are high-pressure machines typically used for cutting harder materials such as metals, ceramics, and composites. They are known for their precision and speed in cutting thick materials. On the other hand, Type II water-jet cutters are low-pressure machines that are more suitable for cutting softer materials like rubber, foam, and plastics. They are commonly used for applications such as gasket cutting and foam shaping.</p></p>
