<p><p>PCR packaging market consists of three main types of materials: PET, PP, and HDPE. PET, or polyethylene terephthalate, is commonly used in bottles and jars due to its transparency and lightweight properties. PP, or polypropylene, is known for its high heat resistance and is commonly used in food packaging. HDPE, or high-density polyethylene, is durable and commonly used in bottles for cleaning products and personal care items. Each material offers unique characteristics that cater to different packaging needs in the market.</p></p>
