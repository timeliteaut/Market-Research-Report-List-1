<p><p>The laxative market consists of various types of products. Hypertonic laxatives work by increasing the water content in the intestines, thus stimulating bowel movements. Lubricating laxatives help soften stool and ease bowel movements. Stimulant laxatives induce contractions in the intestines, promoting bowel movements. Volumetric laxatives work by adding bulk to stool, making it easier to pass. Swelling laxatives swell in the intestines, which stimulates bowel movements. Lastly, there are other types of laxatives available in the market, each with their own unique method of aiding digestion and relieving constipation.</p></p>
