<p><p>Asset tracking software market is divided into two major types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based asset tracking software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It allows users to track and manage their assets from anywhere using any device with an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premise asset tracking software is installed and maintained on the company's own servers. It offers more control and customization options, but requires upfront investment, IT infrastructure, and regular updates and maintenance.</p></p>
