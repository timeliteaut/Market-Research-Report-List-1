<p><p>The infectious wound care management market consists of three main types: wound dressings, bioactives, and devices. Devices include specialized equipment used for wound care, such as negative pressure wound therapy systems or ultrasound devices. These different types of products are designed to aid in the management and treatment of infectious wounds, providing protection, healing, and prevention of infection.</p></p>
