<p><p>Cold form blister foils are pharmaceutical packaging materials that are produced by shifting the plasticity of the formed web and creating cavities through stamping or thermoforming. Cold form blister foils are highly moisture-resistant, light-resistant, and offer excellent barrier properties to protect drugs from atmospheric moisture and gases. They are commonly used to package solid drugs, such as tablets and capsules, due to their ability to keep the drugs safe, extend their shelf life, and maintain their quality.</p><p>The global cold form blister foil market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.3% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing demand for pharmaceutical products, driven by the rising prevalence of diseases and an aging population, is boosting the demand for cold form blister foils. Additionally, stringent regulations regarding the quality, safety, and security of pharmaceutical packaging are also driving the market growth. Cold form blister foils are preferred over other packaging materials due to their durability and ability to protect drugs from external factors.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in the packaging industry, such as the incorporation of features like child resistance, tamper evidence, and user-friendliness, are further propelling the market growth. The increasing focus on sustainable packaging solutions is also expected to drive the demand for eco-friendly cold form blister foils. However, the high cost of production and the availability of alternative packaging solutions may hinder market growth to some extent.</p><p>In conclusion, the global cold form blister foil market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. Factors such as increasing pharmaceutical demand, stringent regulations, technological advancements, and sustainability concerns are driving the market growth.
