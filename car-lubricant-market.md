<p><ul><li>Engine Oil</li><li>Gear Oil</li><li>Transmission Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car lubricant market is divided into three main types: engine oil, gear oil, and transmission fluid. Engine oil is used to reduce friction between moving parts in the engine, ensuring smooth operation and preventing wear and tear. Gear oil is specifically designed for gearboxes in the vehicle to provide lubrication and cooling. Transmission fluid is used in automatic transmissions to provide hydraulic pressure and cooling. Each type of lubricant plays a crucial role in maintaining the performance and longevity of a vehicle's engine and transmission.</p></p>
