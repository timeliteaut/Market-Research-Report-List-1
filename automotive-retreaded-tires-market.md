<p><p>Automotive retreaded tires market can be categorized into high pressure tire and low pressure tire segments. High pressure tires are designed for vehicles that require higher inflation pressure, such as passenger cars and commercial trucks. They are typically used in applications where tires face heavy loads and high speeds. On the other hand, low pressure tires are designed for vehicles that require lower inflation pressure, such as off-road vehicles and agricultural machinery. They are ideal for use in rough terrains where maximum traction is needed.</p></p>
