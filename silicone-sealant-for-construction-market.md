<p><p>Silicone sealants for construction come in different types, including Neutral Cure and Moisture Cure. Neutral Cure sealants are non-acidic and do not corrode metals, making them ideal for use in sensitive applications. On the other hand, Moisture Cure sealants cure in the presence of moisture in the air, making them convenient for quick applications. Both types of sealants offer excellent adhesion and flexibility, making them popular choices in the construction industry for sealing joints and gaps.</p></p>
