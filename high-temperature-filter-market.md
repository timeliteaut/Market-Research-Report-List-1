<p><p>High temperature filters are essential for industries that operate under extreme heat conditions. Different types of high temperature filter materials include PPS, P84, PTFE, Nomex, PSA, and Fiber Glass. PPS filters offer high temperature resistance and chemical compatibility. P84 filters are known for their stable filtration efficiency at high temperatures. PTFE filters have excellent chemical resistance and low friction properties. Nomex filters are suitable for applications with high temperatures and chemical exposure. PSA filters provide high filtration efficiency and durability. Fiber Glass filters offer excellent heat resistance and are cost-effective options.</p></p>
