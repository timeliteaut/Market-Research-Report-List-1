<p><p>Elastic strain sensors can be categorized into resistive, capacitive, and other types. Resistive strain sensors measure strain by changes in resistance due to deformation, while capacitive sensors detect strain through changes in capacitance. Other types of strain sensors may include piezoelectric sensors, optical sensors, or inductive sensors. Each type has its own advantages and applications depending on the specific requirements of the sensing environment. Overall, elastic strain sensors play a crucial role in monitoring structural integrity and performance in various industries.</p></p>
