<p><p>The cable modem equipment market can be categorized into two types: wired and wireless. Wired cable modems require a physical connection through an Ethernet cable to establish an internet connection. They are widely popular due to their reliability and robustness. On the other hand, wireless cable modems use radio waves to transmit and receive data, eliminating the need for physical cables. They offer more flexibility and convenience, allowing users to connect to the internet without being tethered to a specific location.</p></p>
