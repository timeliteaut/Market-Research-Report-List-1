<p><p>Medical rehabilitation robotics market is categorized into two main types: Upper extremity and Lower extremity. Upper extremity robotics focuses on the rehabilitation of patients with upper limb disabilities, such as those resulting from stroke or spinal cord injuries. Lower extremity robotics, on the other hand, targets the rehabilitation of patients with lower limb disabilities, such as those caused by paraplegia or orthopedic conditions. Both types of robotics aim to improve the mobility and function of patients through targeted therapy and exercises.</p></p>
