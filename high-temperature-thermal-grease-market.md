<p><p>High Temperature Thermal Grease Market consists of two main types - Conductivity Thermal Grease and Non-conductive Thermal Grease. Conductivity Thermal Grease is designed to efficiently transfer heat between the heat source and heat sink, thus improving thermal conductivity. On the other hand, Non-conductive Thermal Grease is used in applications where electrical insulation is required to prevent short circuits. These two types cater to different requirements in the electronics and electrical industries, offering solutions for optimal thermal management.</p></p>
