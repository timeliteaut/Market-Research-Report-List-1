<p><p>Hemostatic flow valves are used in medical devices to control the flow of blood or other fluids during procedures. There are different types of hemostatic flow valves available in the market. One-handed flow valves allow for easy control with a single hand. Sideport tubing valves have additional ports for connecting other devices. Straight connector valves provide a straight flow path without any additional features. Y connector valves have a Y-shaped design to allow for branching and merging of fluid flow. These valves cater to different needs in medical procedures.</p></p>
