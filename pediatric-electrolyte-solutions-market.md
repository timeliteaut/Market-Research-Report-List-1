<p><ul><li>Oral</li><li>Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pediatric electrolyte solutions are vital for restoring fluid balance in children suffering from dehydration. There are two main types of these solutions available in the market: oral and injection. Oral electrolyte solutions come in the form of powders or ready-to-use liquids, which can be taken orally to replenish fluids and electrolytes lost due to vomiting or diarrhea. On the other hand, injectable electrolyte solutions are administered intravenously and are used in more severe cases of dehydration or when oral intake is not possible. Both types play crucial roles in managing pediatric dehydration effectively.</p></p>
