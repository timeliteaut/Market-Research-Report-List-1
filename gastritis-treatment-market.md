<p><p>Gastritis is a condition that involves inflammation of the stomach lining, resulting in symptoms like stomach pain, bloating, nausea, vomiting, and indigestion. Gastritis treatment mainly focuses on reducing inflammation, managing symptoms, and promoting healing. The treatment approach may vary based on the underlying cause of gastritis, which can include infection, medication side effects, excessive alcohol consumption, or autoimmune disorders.</p><p>The primary treatment options for gastritis include medication, lifestyle changes, and dietary modifications. Medications like antacids, proton pump inhibitors (PPIs), H2 blockers, and antibiotics are commonly prescribed to reduce stomach acid, eradicate infection (if present), and alleviate symptoms. Lifestyle changes, such as quitting smoking, reducing alcohol consumption, and stress management, are often recommended. Additionally, dietary modifications involve avoiding trigger foods like spicy or acidic foods, caffeine, and alcohol.</p><p>The Gastritis Treatment Market is expected to grow at a significant rate during the forecast period.
