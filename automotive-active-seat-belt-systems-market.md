<p><strong>The Automotive Active Seat Belt Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emergency Locking Retractor</li><li>Automatic Locking Retractor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Emergency Locking Retractor (ELR) seat belts automatically lock in place during sudden deceleration or impact, providing immediate protection to the occupant. This type of system is effective in preventing excessive movement during an accident. On the other hand, Automatic Locking Retractor (ALR) seat belts require the user to manually pull the belt to adjust the length before it locks in place. This system is designed to securely hold the occupant in place, especially useful for securing child safety seats and larger passengers.</p></p>
