<p><p>Electro-Deposited (ED) Copper Foil market can be classified into three main types based on the surface smoothness: High-Temperature Etch (HTE) Copper Foil, Standard (STD) Copper Foil, and Double-Side Treated Foil (DSTF) Copper Foil. HTE Copper Foil is designed for high-temperature applications, STD Copper Foil is widely used in general electronic products, and DSTF Copper Foil has improved adhesion for flexible printed circuit boards. Each type offers specific advantages and is used in different industries based on their unique properties.</p></p>
