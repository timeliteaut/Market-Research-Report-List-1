<p><p>Heavy Commercial Vehicle Brake Caliper market types include Single Piston Caliper and Multi-Piston Caliper. Single Piston Calipers have a single piston that pushes the brake pads against the rotor for braking, while Multi-Piston Calipers have multiple pistons for increased braking power and more consistent pressure distribution. Single Piston Calipers are commonly used in light-duty vehicles, while Multi-Piston Calipers are preferred for heavy commercial vehicles due to their enhanced performance and reliability under heavy braking conditions.</p></p>
