<p><p>The Bluetooth audio chips and modules market consists of two main types: ordinary Bluetooth audio chips and Audio SoC (System on Chip) market. Ordinary Bluetooth audio chips are standalone chips that allow devices to communicate wirelessly over Bluetooth for audio streaming. On the other hand, Audio SoCs integrate multiple functions onto a single chip, combining Bluetooth connectivity with audio processing capabilities. This integration leads to more efficient and compact designs for devices such as headphones, speakers, and other audio products.</p></p>
