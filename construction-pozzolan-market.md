<p><p>Construction Pozzolan is a commonly used mineral admixture in concrete production. There are two main types of Pozzolan available in the market - Artificial Pozzolan and Natural Pozzolan. Artificial Pozzolan is man-made, such as fly ash or silica fume, while Natural Pozzolan is derived from volcanic ash deposits or calcined clay. Both types of Pozzolan improve the durability and strength of concrete by reducing the risk of alkali-silica reaction and increasing resistance to chemical attack. Each type has unique properties that suit different construction needs.</p></p>
