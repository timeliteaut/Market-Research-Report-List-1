<p><p>The Smart Home Machine-to-Machine (M2M) market can be classified into two types: wireless and wired. In the wireless market, smart home devices communicate with each other and the central control unit through wireless technology such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or Zigbee. This allows for easy installation and flexibility in device placement. On the other hand, the wired market involves devices being connected to the central control unit via physical cables like Ethernet or powerline communication. This type ensures a more stable and reliable connection, but may require more complex installation and limits device mobility.</p></p>
