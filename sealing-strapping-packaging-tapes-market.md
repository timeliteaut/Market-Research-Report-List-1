<p><p>Sealing strapping packaging tapes are classified into three main types based on the adhesive used: rubber-based, acrylic, and hot melt adhesive. Rubber-based tapes offer strong adhesion and flexibility, making them ideal for sealing heavy boxes. Acrylic tapes have excellent weather resistance and are commonly used for long-term storage. Hot melt adhesive tapes provide quick adhesion and are suitable for high-volume packaging operations. Each type caters to different needs in the packaging industry, offering unique benefits and applications.</p></p>
