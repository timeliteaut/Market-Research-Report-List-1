<p><p>Transient Suppression Diodes are used to protect electronic circuits from voltage spikes and transients. There are two types of Transient Suppression Diodes in the market - Uni-Polar and Bi-Polar. Uni-Polar diodes conduct current in only one direction, making them suitable for applications involving DC voltage spikes. Bi-Polar diodes can conduct current in both directions, making them ideal for applications with AC voltage spikes. Both types of diodes are essential for ensuring the reliable operation of sensitive electronic equipment in various industries.</p></p>
