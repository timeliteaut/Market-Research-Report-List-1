<p><p>Insulated Copper Tubes are utilized in various industries for their excellent thermal conductivity and corrosion resistance properties. There are two main types of Insulated Copper Tubes: Extruded Copper Tubes and Drawn Copper Tubes. Extruded Copper Tubes are made by forcing a solid copper rod through a die to create the desired shape, while Drawn Copper Tubes are created by pulling a copper rod through a series of dies to decrease its diameter. Both types are commonly used in HVAC, refrigeration, and plumbing applications.</p></p>
