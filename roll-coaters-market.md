<p><p>Roll coaters are used in various industries for applying coatings to different surfaces. There are two main types of roll coaters: hot melt roll coaters and cold adhesive roll coaters. Hot melt roll coaters use melted adhesive that is applied onto a surface using a heated roller. This type of coater is commonly used for applications where quick drying and strong bonding is required. On the other hand, cold adhesive roll coaters use adhesives that do not require heating. These coaters are suitable for applications where heat-sensitive materials are involved or when a slower drying process is preferred.</p></p>
