<p><p>Non-adherent absorbent dressings are categorized into dry dressing and wet dressing markets. Dry dressings are used for wounds with little to no exudate, providing a protective barrier while promoting healing. Wet dressings, on the other hand, are suitable for wounds with moderate to heavy exudate, as they help maintain a moist environment to facilitate healing. Both types of dressings prevent adherence to wound bed, minimize pain during dressing changes, and reduce risk of infection.</p></p>
