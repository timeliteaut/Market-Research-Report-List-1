<p><p>Microbial compound fertilizers can be categorized into different types based on their functions. Nitrogen-fixing fertilizers contain bacteria that convert atmospheric nitrogen into a form that plants can use, improving their growth and health. Phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers contain microbes that break down insoluble phosphorus in the soil, making it more available to plants. Other types of microbial compound fertilizers include those that contain beneficial microbes for overall soil health and plant growth. These products offer natural and sustainable solutions for improving soil fertility and crop yields.</p></p>
