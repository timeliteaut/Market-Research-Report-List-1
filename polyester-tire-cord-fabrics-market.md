<p><p>Polyester tire cord fabrics can be classified into two main types: dipped and undipped. Dipped fabrics are coated with a layer of rubber or other compounds to enhance the overall performance and durability of the tire. On the other hand, undipped fabrics lack this coating and are typically used in applications where the additional layer is not necessary. Both types of fabrics have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the tire manufacturing process.</p></p>
