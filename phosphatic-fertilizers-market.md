<p><p>Phosphatic fertilizers are a type of fertilizers that provide essential nutrients like phosphorous to plants for their growth and development. The market for phosphatic fertilizers is categorized into different types such as Diammonium Phosphate (DAP), Monoammonium Phosphate (MAP), Superphosphate, and Others. DAP and MAP contain both nitrogen and phosphorous, while Superphosphate contains phosphorous in a more soluble form. The "Others" category includes various other phosphate fertilizers that may have different nutrient combinations or formulations to meet specific crop requirements.</p></p>
