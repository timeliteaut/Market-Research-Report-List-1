<p><strong>The Refractory Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaped Refractories</li><li>Unshaped Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refractory market can be broadly categorized into two types - Shaped and Unshaped refractories. Shaped refractories are precast and molded into specific shapes before being fired in kilns. They are commonly used in industrial furnaces, boilers, and kilns. On the other hand, unshaped refractories are monolithic materials that are applied in a plastic or wet state and then dried and fired in place. They are more versatile and can be easily customized to fit different applications in various industries.</p></p>
