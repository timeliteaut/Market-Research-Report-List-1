<p><p>Hemming adhesive market can be categorized into one component and two component adhesives. One component adhesives are pre-mixed with all necessary ingredients and are ready to use straight from the container. They offer convenience and ease of application. Two component adhesives require mixing of two separate parts before application. They are known for their superior strength and performance, making them ideal for applications requiring high bond strength. Both types have their own benefits and are used in various industries based on specific requirements.</p></p>
