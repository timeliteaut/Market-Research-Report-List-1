<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethane Rubber (TPU) market types include Polyester-Based, Polyether-Based, and Others. Polyester-Based TPU offers high mechanical strength and chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications requiring durability. Polyether-Based TPU is known for its excellent flexibility, impact resistance, and resistance to microbial growth, making it ideal for medical and automotive applications. The Others category includes TPU blends with other materials to enhance specific properties, such as flame retardancy or UV resistance, catering to a wider range of applications in various industries.</p></p>
