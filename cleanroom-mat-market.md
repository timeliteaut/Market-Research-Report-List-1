<p><p>Cleanroom mat market consists of two main types: temporary tacky mats and permanent tacky mats. Temporary tacky mats are disposable adhesive mats placed at the entrance of cleanrooms to trap dirt and debris from shoes. They are replaced frequently to maintain cleanliness. Permanent tacky mats are reusable mats with sticky surfaces that can be cleaned and maintained for long periods. Both types of mats are essential in cleanroom environments to prevent contamination and maintain high levels of cleanliness.</p></p>
