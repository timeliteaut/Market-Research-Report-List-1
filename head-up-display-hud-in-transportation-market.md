<p><strong>The Head-Up Display (HUD) in Transportation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Windshield-Projected HUDs</li><li>Combiner-Projected HUDs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Head-Up Display (HUD) technology in transportation is categorized into two main types: windshield-projected HUDs and combiner-projected HUDs. Windshield-projected HUDs display information directly onto the windshield, allowing the driver to see relevant data without taking their eyes off the road. Combiner-projected HUDs use a separate transparent screen, or combiner, to display information in the driver's line of sight. Both types of HUDs enhance driving safety and convenience by providing essential information such as speed, navigation directions, and vehicle alerts.</p></p>
