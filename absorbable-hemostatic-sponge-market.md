<p><p>The absorbable hemostatic sponge market consists of two main types: skin gelatin and bone gelatin. Skin gelatin sponges are made from animal-derived collagen and are typically used in surgeries involving the closure of skin wounds or incisions. These sponges aid in the clotting process by promoting the aggregation of platelets and the formation of fibrin clots. On the other hand, bone gelatin sponges are primarily used in dental and orthopedic surgeries to control bleeding and facilitate bone healing. They are made from bovine or porcine collagen and help in maintaining hemostasis during procedures.</p></p>
