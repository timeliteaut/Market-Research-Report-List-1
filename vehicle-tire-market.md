<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radial tires have cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing better stability, fuel efficiency, and longer tread life. They are commonly used in passenger vehicles and commercial trucks. Bias tires have cords that run at an angle to the direction of travel, providing a smoother ride but less stability and fuel efficiency. They are typically used in off-road vehicles, agricultural equipment, and vintage cars. Both types of tires have their own advantages and are suitable for different types of vehicles and applications.</p></p>
