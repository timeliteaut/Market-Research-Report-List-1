Powder rGO is typically used for bulk manufacturing in industries such as electronics and energy storage. Flake rGO, on the other hand, is popular in applications requiring a large surface area, like coatings and composites. Liquor rGO is a liquid suspension that is commonly used in research labs and for small-scale applications. Each type offers unique properties and benefits depending on the desired end use.</p></p>
