<p><p>The thin-film drug delivery market consists of various types, including oral thin film, transdermal film, and others. Oral thin film refers to a strip that is placed on the tongue, dissolves quickly, and releases medication into the bloodstream for faster absorption. Transdermal film works by applying a patch to the skin, which slowly releases the drug into the bloodstream over time. The "others" category covers thin films used for ocular or nasal drug delivery, providing targeted treatment options. These innovative delivery methods offer convenience, improved adherence, and enhanced efficacy.</p></p>
