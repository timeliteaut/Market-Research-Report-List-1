<p><p>Medical Quality Management Systems (MQMS) are tools used to assess, maintain, and improve the quality of healthcare services. The market for these systems can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based MQMS utilizes internet-based platforms, allowing users to access and manage data remotely, while on-premises MQMS is hosted and managed within the healthcare facility's local infrastructure. Both types offer advantages and disadvantages. Cloud-based systems provide flexibility, scalability, and cost-efficiency. On the other hand, on-premises systems offer greater control, security, and customization options. Ultimately, the choice depends on the specific needs and preferences of healthcare organizations.</p></p>
