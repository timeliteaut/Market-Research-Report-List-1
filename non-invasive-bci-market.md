<p><p>Non-Invasive BCI technology includes various types such as EEG, fMRI, NIRS, MEG, and ECoG. EEG measures brain activity through electrodes placed on the scalp, while fMRI uses magnetic fields to track blood flow in the brain. NIRS monitors changes in blood oxygen levels, MEG records magnetic fields generated by neural activity, and ECoG involves placing electrodes directly on the brain's surface. Each type offers unique advantages for studying brain function non-invasively, making them valuable tools for research and medical applications.</p></p>
