<p><p>Automotive transparent silicone market types include elastomers, resins, gels, and fluids. Elastomers are rubber-like materials known for their elasticity and flexibility, making them ideal for gaskets and seals. Resins are typically used in adhesive and coating applications due to their durability and high temperature resistance. Gels have a soft, jelly-like consistency and are commonly used for damping and cushioning purposes. Fluids are liquid silicone materials used as lubricants or sealants in automotive applications for their heat resistance and low toxicity.</p></p>
