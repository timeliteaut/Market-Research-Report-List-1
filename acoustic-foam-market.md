<p><p>Acoustic foam comes in various types, each designed to serve specific purposes in soundproofing and acoustics. Acoustic Wedge foam features a wedge-shaped design to absorb sound waves effectively. Acoustic Pyramid foam has a pyramid shape that diffuses sound and reduces echoes. Acoustic Eggcrate foam has an egg crate pattern for sound absorption. Acoustic Spade foam has a spade-shaped design for optimal sound absorption. Acoustic Wave foam has a wavy pattern for scattering sound waves. Acoustic Grid foam features a grid pattern for reducing noise reflections.</p></p>
