<p><p>Acrylic Lenses Market consists of two main types: Monofocal IOLs and Premium IOLs. Monofocal IOLs are traditional lenses that offer fixed focus at a set distance, typically for either near or distance vision. Premium IOLs, on the other hand, provide additional features such as accommodation, astigmatism correction, or multifocal capabilities. These advanced lenses aim to reduce the need for glasses after cataract surgery and provide a more customized vision correction solution for patients.</p></p>
