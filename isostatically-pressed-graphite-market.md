<p><p>Isostatically Pressed Graphite Market consists of two main types - CIP Method and Vibration Molding Method. The CIP Method involves the use of a die to compact the graphite powder under high pressure, resulting in a dense and uniform product. On the other hand, the Vibration Molding Method uses vibrations to compact the graphite powder, resulting in a more porous and lightweight product. Both methods have their own advantages and are used in various industries for different applications.</p></p>
