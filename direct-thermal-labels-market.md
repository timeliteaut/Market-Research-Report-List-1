<p><p>Direct thermal labels are available in two main types: paper and plastic. Paper direct thermal labels are commonly used for short-term applications such as shipping labels, price tags, and receipts. They are cost-effective and eco-friendly. On the other hand, plastic direct thermal labels are more durable and water-resistant, making them suitable for long-term and outdoor applications like food packaging and asset tracking. Both types of direct thermal labels offer high-quality printing results without the need for ink or ribbons.</p></p>
