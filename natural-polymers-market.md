<p><p>Natural polymers market can be categorized into four main types: cellulose ethers, exudate & vegetable gums, starch & fermentation products, and other polymers. Cellulose ethers are derivatives of cellulose and widely used in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and construction. Exudate & vegetable gums are natural plant extracts that serve as thickening and stabilizing agents. Starch & fermentation products are derived from crops like corn and wheat and find applications in food, textiles, and paper industries. Other polymers include proteins and alginates.</p></p>
