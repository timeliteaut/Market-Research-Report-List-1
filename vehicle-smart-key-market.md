<p><p>Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) allow drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without removing the key from their pocket or purse. This technology uses sensors to detect the key's presence, automatically unlocking the doors when the driver approaches. Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES) allow drivers to unlock their vehicle from a distance using a remote control. Both PKES and RKES offer convenience and security to drivers, making it easier to access and operate their vehicles.</p></p>
