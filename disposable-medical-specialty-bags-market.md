<p><p>Disposable medical specialty bags are specific types of bags used in healthcare settings for various medical procedures. Anesthesia breathing bags are used to deliver anesthetic gases to patients during surgery. Bile collection bags collect bile from the gallbladder. Ostomy bags are used to collect waste from a surgically created opening in the body for intestinal or urinary elimination. Resuscitation bags provide oxygen during emergency situations. Blood bags are used for collecting, storing, and transporting blood and its components. CAPD bags are used in continuous ambulatory peritoneal dialysis. Enema bags provide fluid for rectal cleaning. Enteral feeding bags deliver liquid nutrition to patients. IV bags are used for intravenous delivery of fluids and medications. Urinary collection bags collect urine from patients.</p></p>
