<p><p>Front Radar Sensor Market Types:</p><p>1. Ultrasonic Radar: This type of radar sensor uses sound waves to detect objects in its surrounding environment. This technology is commonly used in parking assist systems and collision avoidance systems in vehicles.</p><p>2. Millimeter Wave Radar: Millimeter wave radar sensors operate at higher frequencies than ultrasonic radar, providing greater accuracy and range. They are commonly used in advanced driver assistance systems and autonomous vehicles for improved detection capabilities.</p><p>3. Lidar Radar: Lidar (Light Detection and Ranging) radar sensors use laser pulses to detect objects and create high-resolution 3D maps of their surroundings. This technology is used in autonomous vehicles for precise object recognition and navigation.</p></p>
