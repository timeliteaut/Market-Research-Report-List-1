Low porosity metals have smaller pores with higher density and strength, making them suitable for applications requiring precision and durability. Medium porosity metals offer a balance between porosity and strength, suitable for various applications. High porosity metals have larger pores, providing high permeability and lightweight properties, ideal for filtration and insulation purposes.</p></p>
