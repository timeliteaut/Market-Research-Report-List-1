<p><p>Negative photoresist developer is a chemical solution used for developing negative photoresist coatings in photolithography processes. Negative photoresists are sensitive to UV light, and the areas exposed to light become insoluble while the unexposed areas can be dissolved away with the developer solution. This process allows for the creation of high-resolution patterns on substrates like silicon wafers for semiconductor manufacturing.</p><p>The negative photoresist developer market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
