<p><p>Audio switches are devices used to route audio signals from multiple sources to a single output, or vice versa. The two main types of audio switches are Audio Signal Switches and Audio Input Switches. Audio Signal Switches allow users to select between different audio sources connected to a single output, while Audio Input Switches allow users to switch between multiple input sources connected to a single output. These switches are commonly used in home audio systems, recording studios, and live sound setups to easily manage audio signal routing.</p></p>
