<p><ul><li>Sieve Dehydration</li><li>Centrifugal Dehydration</li><li>Filtration Dehydration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical dewatering equipment market includes different types such as sieve dehydration, centrifugal dehydration, and filtration dehydration. Sieve dehydration involves the use of a physical barrier to separate liquid from solid material. Centrifugal dehydration utilizes spinning motion to separate liquids from solids. Filtration dehydration involves passing a mixture through a filter to separate the liquid from solid particles. Each type of equipment offers unique advantages and is used in various industries for dewatering processes.</p></p>
