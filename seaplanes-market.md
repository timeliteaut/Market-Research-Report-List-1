Flying Boats have a boat-like fuselage which enables them to land on water surfaces. Amphibians are versatile aircraft that can operate on both water and land, equipped with retractable landing gear and floats. Each type of seaplane serves different purposes and caters to specific needs in industries such as tourism, transportation, and military.</p></p>
