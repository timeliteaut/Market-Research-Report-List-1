<p><p>The IoT motion sensor market comprises various types of sensors including Passive Infrared (PIR), Microwave, Ultrasound, Tomography, and others. PIR sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by moving objects, while Microwave sensors use radio waves to detect motion. Ultrasound sensors emit high-frequency sound waves to detect movement, and Tomography sensors create 3D images to track motion. Other types of motion sensors may use different technologies such as vibration or pressure sensors to detect movement in various applications.</p></p>
