<p><p>Copper foil for PCB market types can be classified into two main categories: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil is produced through an electrolysis process, while rolled copper foil is manufactured by rolling copper sheets. These two types can further be categorized based on copper thickness, with options including less than 10 um, 11-30um, 31-50um, and more than 51um. Each type of copper foil offers unique characteristics and benefits for different applications in the PCB industry.</p></p>
