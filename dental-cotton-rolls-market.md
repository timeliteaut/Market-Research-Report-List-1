<p><p>Dental cotton rolls are available in two main market types: sterile and non-sterile. These rolls are specifically sterilized before packaging, reducing the risk of infection. On the other hand, non-sterile cotton rolls do not go through the sterilization process and may contain microorganisms. While they may be used for various purposes in the dental field, they are not considered suitable for sterile procedures or applications requiring a high level of cleanliness.</p></p>
